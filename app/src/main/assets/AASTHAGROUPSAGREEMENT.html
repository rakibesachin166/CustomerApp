For the purpose of this Agreement, the following terms shall have the meanings ascribed to them:

  1. "ASG"; refers to Aastha Saksham Groups, having its website www.aasthagroups.com" and email address hello@aasthagroups.com.

  6. "[User Role]" refers to the designation held by the [User Role] under this Agreement, who will have control over specific employees assigned to work within the designated block.

  1. SCOPE OF SERVICES

  1. [User Role]'s Role and Designation

The [User Role] shall serve as the [User Role] Chairman and will be responsible for overseeing and managing the operations within the assigned block. The [User Role] will be given control over the employees working under this block, including the authority to supervise and manage their day-to-day activities.

  1. Car for Management

The Company agrees to provide a car to the [User Role] for management purposes, for which the Company will bear the down payment. The Company will also bear the EMI for the car until the [User Role]'s total monthly income exceeds INR Fifty Thousand. After the [User Role]'s income reaches this threshold, the [User Role] shall be responsible for bearing the REST AMOUNT of the car.

  1. Employee Support

The Company will assign certain employees to assist the [User Role] in adding consumers within the block. The [User Role] shall have control over these employees but must ensure compliance with the Company's policies and guidelines.

  1. SERVICE CHARGE AND PAYMENTS TERMS

  1. Service and Processing Charge

The [User Role] shall pay a non-refundable service and processing charge of INR Five lakhs to the Company before the commencement of services.

  1. Payment for Car EMI

The Company agrees to bear the EMI for the car provided to the [User Role] until the [User Role]'s monthly income exceeds INR Fifty Thousand. Once the [User Role]'s income exceeds this threshold, the [User Role] shall take full responsibility for paying the remaining EMIs.

  1. Company's Control Over Accounts

The Company shall have full control over all accounts related to the block operations at all times. This includes the authority to access, monitor, and audit accounts to ensure compliance with Company policies and standards.

  1. [User Role]'S RESPONSIBILITIES

  1. Supervision of Employees

The [User Role] shall be responsible for supervising and managing the employees assigned to the block. The [User Role] shall ensure that the employees perform their duties efficiently and in compliance with Company policies.

  1. Supervision of Vendors

The [User Role] shall be responsible for supervising and managing the vendors assigned to the block. The [User Role] shall ensure that the vendors perform their duties efficiently and in compliance with Company policies.

  1. Performance and Reporting

The [User Role] shall provide regular reports to the Company regarding the performance of the block operations, consumer addition, and any other relevant information as required by the Company.

  1. Compliance

The [User Role] shall comply with all applicable laws, rules, and regulations and ensure that no unlawful activities are conducted under the [User Role]'s supervision.

  1. Use of Car

The [User Role] agrees to use the car provided for management purposes only. The [User Role] shall not engage in any misuse or unauthorized use of the car. Any damages or liabilities arising out of misuse of the car shall be borne solely by the [User Role].

  1. COMPANY'S CONTROL AND RIGHTS

  1. Company's Right to Control Accounts

The Company retains the right to control all accounts related to the block operations, including the authority to audit and review financial transactions and records at any time. The [User Role] shall provide full access to such accounts upon the Company's request.

  1. NON-REFUNDABLE CLAUSE

The Parties agree that the service and processing charge of INR Five Lakhs only paid by the [User Role] is non-refundable under any circumstances. This includes, but is not limited to, termination of this Agreement for any reason.

  1. INCOME THRESHOLD AND EMI RESPONSIBILITY

  1. Income Threshold

The [User Role]'s income shall be monitored, and once the total monthly income exceeds INR Fifty Thousand, the [User Role] shall bear the responsibility for paying the EMI for the car provided by the Company.
